Madi Alexander

Hello! I’m Madi Alexander. I am a political science and mass communications senior at Oklahoma City University. My emphases are in public relations and international affairs with a minor in Spanish.

Currently I am the online editing intern at The Oklahoman. I work with the digital desk to manage the NewsOK website, social media presence and other digital products for The Oklahoman.

I was a member of OCU’s student media for four semesters, working as the web editor and graphic designer. During the 2013 legislative session, I covered the Oklahoma legislature as an intern for The Journal Record Legislative Report.

This summer I briefly worked for CNN after the Moore tornado as a production assistant for several shows, including AC360 and The Situation Room. Thanks to a grant from the Oklahoma Newspaper Foundation, I spent the remainder of the summer as an intern at the Shawnee News-Star, covering everything from rodeos to rainfall to tractor pulls.
