Madi Alexander

Hello! I’m Madi Alexander. I am a multimedia journalist, native Oklahoman, adventurer, tea-drinker and so much more.

In the fall, I will begin pursuing a master’s degree in journalism at the University of Missouri in Columbia. I have a bachelor’s degree in mass communications and political science with a minor in Spanish from Oklahoma City University.

Previously I worked as the web intern at NewsOK.com/The Oklahoman, where I helped the digital desk manage the NewsOK website, social media presence and other multimedia products. Before that, I interned at the Shawnee News-Star in Shawnee, Okla., covering everything from tractor pulls to city government to rodeos.

I also spent six semesters as a part of OCU’s student media and another semester covering the Oklahoma legislature for The Journal Record Legislative Report. Last year I was a production assistant for CNN during their coverage of the May 20 tornado that devastated my hometown of Moore, Okla.

Video, web, print, multimedia. I do it all. Check out my work below or head over to the travels section to check out some of my photography from my adventures overseas.

In my free time, I enjoy cooking, hanging out with my two dogs and making plans for my next adventure. Whether it’s Istanbul, Austin or rural Oklahoma, I love traveling and exploring new places. I like learning to use new technology and tools that make me a better journalist, storyteller and writer. I love the Middle East, chai, open records, parliamentary procedure, technology and sci-fi. With an international background, I have always had a passion for language. I learned to speak Spanish at an advanced level and am working on learning Arabic, which is about as difficult as you’d imagine. If you have questions or comments, feel free to contact me on one of the many social media sites.
